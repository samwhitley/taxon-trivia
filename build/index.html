Welcome to Taxon Trivia!

How to Play

Taxonomy is the categorization of living things into groups based on shared characteristics and common descent. To start this game, you pick a taxon, or grouping of organisms. Next, you are presented with photographs of representative animals and are given the name of a smaller grouping within the taxon. Your goal is to choose the animal that belongs to this smaller group.

About this Game

Being able to identify key features and relationships between taxa is an important skill for those who wish to study a particular group in detail. All content for this game is sourced from the Encyclopedia of Life API - none of the animal pictures or written descriptions are hard coded into this app. Therefore, this game can easily be repurposed with new categories, extending the educational possibilities.
